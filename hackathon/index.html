ABOUT

CIA Hackathon aims at bringing together engineers, designers, and students to hack and build life-changing solutions using rapid prototyping techniques. CIA Hackathon is a 24-hour event which will be held at Atria Institute of Technology on the x and y of April, 2020. The vision of CIA Hackathon is to identify and solve problems in improving the lives of the common man and harness technology to creatively demonstrate ideas.

Tracks/Themes

Mobility

Mobility is changing rapidly and we as a civilisation are at the cusp of electric transformation at scale. But this domain also has few questions unanswered at scale. You can solve any problem pertaining to the problems of mobility. Electric Vehicles, Autonomous Vehicles, Automotive Battery Management Systems, Aerodynamics, Avionics, Unmanned Aerial Systems, and Mass Transportation are some of the streams that lead down this path.

Digital transformation

Technology is revolutionizing every domain out there. Artificial Intelligence is lurking at various use cases to solve problems at scale. AR/VR is changing our perspective of the world around and Data Science is helping us harness the 21st century data set. This theme pertains to these technologies that transform anything and everything in their path.

Healthcare

Out of all domains technology is having an impact, the most important one is Healthcare. From helping hospitals to self diagnosis, technology can and will eventually increase its presence in the domain of heath. You are encouraged to solve any problem that can have digital and technological applications in this domain

EdTech

Traditional learning is quickly getting out-of-context. Interactive and output driven concepts have to be employed to teach and equip students for 21st century requirements. Design any technological solution to change and/or transform the current education system. Your novelty is the need of the hour !!

FinTech

With over 2 lakh crore in digital UPI payments, India is quickly moving to a digital, cashless system. Design and build solutions that harness this trend, makes sure that the rural population are not left out and privacy and security are at the forefront. You may also build fintech solutions on blockchain technologies.
